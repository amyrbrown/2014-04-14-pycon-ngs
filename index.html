General Information

What: This workshop is a master class in next-generation sequencing based on those run at Michigan State University for the past several summers.

Who: The course is aimed at postgraduate students and other scientists who are familiar with basic concepts in both programming and bioinformatics. Note that the workshop is open to everyone, not just PyCon attendees.
